How to play

Step 1: The Picker

  • Action: Choose a prompt for the next player.
  • 💡 Tip: The prompt is not a secret! You can discuss it or clarify what you meant.

Step 2: The Responder

  • Action: Type 2 answers that fit the prompt. Only after that, you will see the secret question.
  • 🤫 Rule: Keep absolute silence! Do not say your words out loud and keep your final choice a strict secret.

Step 3: The Guessers

  • Action: Guess which option the Responder chose!
  • Need a hint? Spend points to use abilities:
    • 🎲 Random: Opens random letters.
    • 📏 Length: Shows word length & first letters.
