PASS PHONE

How to play (Easy Guide)

Step 1: The Picker

Read the secret question. Choose 1 short tip (Prompt) for the next player.

PLAYER 1 SEES:
Question: Would you rather eat ___ or ___?
[Select Prompt Button] ➜ "Name 2 fast foods"

Step 2: The Responder

You see ONLY the prompt. Type 2 answers. Then, choose your favorite option.

PLAYER 2 SEES:
Prompt: "Name 2 fast foods"
Input 1: PIZZA | Input 2: BURGER
[Secret Choice] ➜ I prefer: PIZZA

Step 3: The Guessers

Other players must guess: What did Player 2 choose? Use buttons below to open hidden letters. First choice is FREE, second costs 15 points! ⚠️ Note: Depending on the words written in this round, some buttons might lock or open fewer letters. You can tap the small "?" icon if you want to see exactly what that button will do right now.

GUESSERS SEE:
Would you rather eat P * * Z * or B * * G * *?
[Tap your guess] ➜ Win +50 points if correct!
Step 1 / 3

ℹ️ 2 Players: You play with each other.
ℹ️ 3+ Players: The phone passes to the next person in a circle.
