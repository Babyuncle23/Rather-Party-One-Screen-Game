Open Circle

This game is made for fun, connection, and good times — never for causing genuine distress or making anyone feel unsafe.

Take a deep breath. Use this timeout to check in with each other, talk openly, or simply shift the conversation. Your friendship matters more than any points.

Recommended for all players to read before starting.

How to play (Easy Guide)

Step 1: The Picker

Read the secret question. Choose 1 pre-made prompt or write a custom one for the next player.

PLAYER 1 SEES:
Question: Would you rather eat ___ or ___?
[Select Prompt] ➜ "Name 2 fast foods"

Step 2: The Responder

You see ONLY the prompt. Type 2 different answers, then secretly select your preferred option.

PLAYER 2 SEES:
Prompt: "Name 2 fast foods"
Input 1: PIZZA | Input 2: BURGER
[Secret Choice] ➜ I prefer: PIZZA

Step 3: The Guessers

Guess what Player 2 chose! Word lengths are hidden behind separators. Spend points on abilities to open letters (1st is FREE, 2nd costs 15 pts). In 2-player matches, once all reveals are spent, an Oral Hint option appears!

GUESSERS SEE:
ANSWER 1 (1 word)
P || Z ||
[Tap your guess] ➜ Win +50 points!
