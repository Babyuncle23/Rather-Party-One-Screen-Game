Quick game flow

1. The active player reads the secret question and picks a prompt for the next player.

2. The next player sees only the prompt, types two different answers, then chooses which answer fits.

3. Other players take turns guessing the chosen answer after letters are revealed.

Prompt: “Pick one option”
Answer A: “Ride a dragon”
Answer B: “Swim with dolphins”
Then: secret question with one answer hidden

If only two players are in the game, the prompt goes to the other player. With more players, the prompt goes to the next player in order.
